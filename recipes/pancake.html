Pancakes

Description

One of my favourite morning feeds. Sweet to kick start your day!

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3.5 tsp baking powder
  • 1.25 cups milk
  • 1 tbsp white sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3 tbsp buttler, melted
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Sift flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt together in a large bowl.
  2. Make a well in the center and add milk, melted butter, and egg; mix until smooth
  3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or pan over medium-high heat
  4. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry, about 2 to 3 minutes
  5. Flip and cook until browned on the other side. Repeat with remaining batter
