Crème Brûlée

Description

One of my favourite desserts. The rich and creamy texture is unworldy!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 3 large egg yolks
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar, separated

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 175 degrees Celsius
  2. Whisk together cream and 3 tablespoons sugar in a microwave-safe bowl until combined
  3. Cook in the microwave for 1 to 2 minutes, until warm
  4. Take cream mixture out and whisk again to dissolve sugar. Whisk in egg yolks and vanilla until smooth.
  5. Pour cream mixture into 2 bowls set in a roasting pan
  6. Pour enough hot water into the pan to reach halfway up the sides of the bowls
  7. Bake for ~50 min until custard is set but slightly jiggly in the center when shaken
  8. Remove bowls from hot water and chill in the refrigerator until cold, at least 2 hours
  9. Sprinkle 1 tablespoon sugar evenly over the top of each dessert
  10. Use a kitchen torch to melt and lightly toast sugar topping until brown and bubbly
