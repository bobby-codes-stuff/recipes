Teriyaki Sauce!

Description

This is a versatile sauce that can be used with any meat and vegetables. Good for meal prep.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Sake
  • 1 cup Mirin
  • 1 cup Soy Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Cooking Oil

Optional Condiments

  • 2 cloves Garlic
  • 1 Ginger
  • 1 tbsp Sesame Seeds
  • Paparika

Steps

  1. Heat up saucepot with a low-medium heat
  2. Add oil into pan once heated
  3. Place garlic and ginger along with any other condiments into the saucepot

    4. Bloom the aromatics in the oil - best carrier of aromatic flavour

  4. Stir condiments for ~3 min, eavenly distributing heat and prevent burning
  5. Add in the sake, mirin, and soy sauce in equal parts (1:1:1 ratio)
  6. Turn the heat up to a gentle simmer (small bubbles), let the alcohol cook off and intensify flavour
  7. Add sugar/honey if sauce is not sweet
  8. If sauce is not thick, create a cornstarch slurry and mix into the teriyaki mixture
    1. Mix a 2:1 ratio of water to cornstarch
    2. Mix until mixture is smooth
  9. Add in sesame seeds and mix and stir until evenly distributed
  10. Pour mixture into a sealed jar to use on your favourite meat and veges!
